Frequently Asked Questions:

1. INTRODUCTION

Q: Why to submit to IJCT?

A: IJCT is an international peer reviewed, open access, online journal

Q: What is the ISSN number of IJCT?

A:  e-ISSN: 2394-2231

2. SUBMISSION

Q: When should we submit our papers?

A: Authors can submit the manuscripts at anytime by through Mail to editorijctjournal@gmail.com. It will be published in forthcoming issue, if accepted.

Q: Where can I find Manuscript Template?

A: You can find the Manuscript Template in downloads menu

Q: Where can I find Authors Guidelines?

A: You can find the Author Guidelines in Author Desk  Menu.

Q: Where can I find Copyright Form?

A: If your paper is selected, download the Copyright Form from Downloads menu, fill it and send it to online submission link.

Q: How do I submit my paper?

A: Submit your manuscript electronically through Mail to editorijctjournal@gmail.com .To submit a manuscript electronically you need to convert your files to MS-Word format (.doc or docx).

Q: When will I receive an acknowledgment email for my submission?

A: Online Paper submissions are acknowledged Immediately & email submission within 24 hours. In case the author do not receive any acknowledgment for a submitted paper, an email can be sent to editor IJCT

Q: How many authors are allowed per paper? Is there a maximum limit for the number of authors?

A: Yes. At present, maximum of FOUR authors are allowed per paper.

3. PUBLICATION

Q: Are IRJET published articles available for free?

A: Yes, all the papers published by IJCT are available for download for free. We are here to support the Open Access.

Q: How often is the Journal published?

A: Journal publishes once in a month, total of 6 issues per year.

Q: My paper has been published, but I cannot find it on the Web.

A: All published papers are normally indexed within 5-6 days from the date of publication and thereafter should normally be visible on the Web within 1 week.

Q: How much do I have to pay for Publication Fee?

A: IJCT Publication Fee for Accepted Papers:

For Standard Author from India: INR 1280

For Standard Author from other countries: USD 61

Q: How to deposit publication fees?

A: If your paper is accepted for publication, you can choose one of the following options:

Option-1:You can do the direct deposit in any of the bank which is having online transfer facility and send the scanned copy of pay slip.

Option-2: You can do the online NEFT transfer by using IFSC code and send the transaction ID. We recommend this option, which is easy, fast and convenient at any time.
