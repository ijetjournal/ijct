Reviewing Process

IJCT has introduced scholarly peer review of Five days. Scholary peer review is the process of subjecting as authors? scholarly work, research or ideas to the scrutiny of others who are experts in the same field, before a paper describing this work is published in a journal. The work may be accepted, considered acceptable with revisions, or rejected. Peer review requires a community of experts in a given field, who are qualified and able to perform review.

  1. 1.Each papers will be reviewed by at least one expert.
  2. 2.Author submits manuscript.
  3. 3.Editor's Assistant performs quality checks to ensure author's manuscript meets journal submission requirements.
  4. 4.Potential Reviewers are assigned and contacted.
  5. 5.Reviewers review manuscript.
  6. 6.The final decision and publication will be made within 5 days.
  7. 7.Editor makes decision to accept, request revisions, or reject.
  8. 8.If decision is a revision, Author revises manuscript and submits revision.
  9. 9.Editor makes new decision.
  10. 10. Author contacted with decision
  11. 11. Steps 8?10 repeated as necessary until Editor makes final decision.
  12. 12. When paper is accepted, manuscript enters production system..
